# Divani Mods v1.2.5
Some new Impostor roles and modifiers join Divani Mods!
Most Starlight checks are done, shouldn't take long now!
## Added

### Added Role: Mosquito (Impostor Power)

Launch Mosquito Drones to targets to sting them to death. Choose between
auto-targeting the furthest player or picking a target with a selection tablet;
charge-based with optional Aimbot Mode.

### Added Role: Obfuscator (Impostor Power)

Swap votes between two players. This effect always comes after a regular Swapper.

### Added Role: Cunctator (Impostor Concealing)

Bodies of those the Cunctator kills only appear after a configurable delay.

### Added Modifier: Skilled (Crewmate Utility)

Fix two-part sabotages (reactor, oxygen) on your own without a second player.

### Added Modifier: Strong (Crewmate Passive)

Cannot be guessed in meetings.

### Added Modifier: Incompetent (Crewmate Passive)

Cannot fix sabotages.
