# Divani Mods v1.2.3

## Added

### Added Role: Domesmith (Crewmate Protective)

Use your dome shield to protect groups (Limited use)
Wacht out for Ruthless still, they show no mercy!

### Added Role: Summoner (Impostor Power) - Revenant (Impostor Afterlife)

Bring the dead to life, and bring them to your side! Select a dead shipmate in a meeting to summon them as a Ghost Impostor Role (Revenant)
A revenant can kill, vent but also be clicked like a Haunter, turning them into a impostor Ghost
Once revent(has been revenant) you can no longer see the roles of alive non-imps

### Added Modifier: Sprout (Crewamte Utility)

One time ability to gain a modifier from a dead body. Non-allowed modifiers will turn into valid ones (Thief like)
